Overview

What Our Compliance Management Service Covers

Labour law in India is detailed and constantly evolving, and non-compliance carries real financial and legal risk. Aryncare's compliance management service keeps your statutory registers, filings and documentation audit-ready at all times, across PF, ESI, minimum wages and the Contract Labour Act.

  • PF, ESI & Contract Labour Act filings
  • Statutory registers maintained
  • Monthly compliance reports
