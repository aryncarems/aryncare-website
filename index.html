FAQs

Common Questions

Quick answers about working with Aryncare.

What is manpower outsourcing and how does Aryncare help my business? +
Manpower outsourcing means partnering with a specialist agency like Aryncare to recruit, deploy, pay and statutorily manage your workforce, so you get skilled staff without the administrative and compliance burden of direct employment.
Which cities and regions does Aryncare operate in? +
We are headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and provide staffing and workforce management support across Tamil Nadu with PAN India deployment capability for multi-location clients.
How quickly can Aryncare deploy staff after I raise a requirement? +
For most contract staffing and industrial labour requirements, we deploy verified candidates within 48-72 hours. Bulk or specialised hiring may take slightly longer depending on the role.
Is the workforce deployed by Aryncare covered under PF and ESI? +
Yes. Every employee deployed on Aryncare's payroll is registered under PF and ESI as applicable, with monthly statutory filings and compliance reports shared with clients.
